## About DataTalks.Club

DataTalks.Club is a community of people who work with data. We organize free courses, learning groups, events, and discussions about data engineering, machine learning, MLOps, LLMs, and related topics.

## Short Description

DataTalks.Club is a community for data practitioners to learn, share knowledge, and grow together through free courses, events, and peer support.

## Usage

Use the DataTalks.Club logo when referring to the community, courses, events, or projects. Do not modify the logo colors, stretch the logo, or place it on backgrounds where it is hard to read.
