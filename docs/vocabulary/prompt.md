## Examples

Prompts can range from simple questions to complex instructions with examples and context.

### Simple Prompt
```
What is the capital of France?
```

### Complex Prompt
```
You are an expert travel guide. Create a 3-day itinerary for Paris, France for a family with two children (ages 8 and 12). Include family-friendly attractions, restaurant recommendations, and transportation options. The family is visiting in June and has a moderate budget.
```
