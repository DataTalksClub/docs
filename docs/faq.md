# Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to the DataTalks.Club FAQ. Here you'll find answers to common questions about our courses and community.

## General Questions

### Are DataTalks.Club courses really free?

Yes! All our zoomcamps are completely free. We believe education should be accessible to everyone.

### How do I register for a course?

Visit the registration link for your chosen course:
- [Machine Learning Zoomcamp](https://airtable.com/appflP5cuR8bD5MIm/shryxwLd0COOEaqXo)
- [Data Engineering Zoomcamp](https://airtable.com/appzbS8Pkg9PL254a/shr6oVXeQvSI5HuWD)
- [MLOps Zoomcamp](https://airtable.com/appYdhA23GVZd1iN2/shrCb8y6eTbPKwSTL)
- [LLM Zoomcamp](https://airtable.com/appPPxkgYLH06Mvbw/shr7WtxHEPXxaui0Q)

### What is the time commitment?

Courses vary from 6-16 weeks. Most students spend 10-15 hours per week on lectures, homework, and projects.

## Course Format

### Do I need to attend live sessions?

Most of our courses are self-paced with pre-recorded lectures. There are no mandatory live sessions, though we occasionally host optional workshops and Q&A sessions.

### What will I receive upon completion?

Students who complete all homework and the final project receive a certificate of completion.

### Can I take multiple courses at once?

Yes, but we recommend starting with one course to ensure you can dedicate enough time to learning and completing assignments.

## Technical Requirements

### What skills do I need?

Basic requirements include:
- Python programming knowledge
- Command line familiarity
- Basic understanding of Git

Some courses have additional prerequisites - check the specific course page for details.

### What computer do I need?

Any modern computer with internet access will work. All coursework can be done using free tools and cloud services.
