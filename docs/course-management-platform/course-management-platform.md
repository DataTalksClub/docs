# Course Management Platform

Welcome to the DataTalks.Club course management platform. This platform helps you track your progress, submit assignments, and engage with the course community.

## Platform Features

### Dashboard
- Track your progress through modules
- View upcoming deadlines
- Access course materials

### Homework Submission
- Submit your homework solutions
- Receive automatic grading
- Track your scores on the leaderboard

### Community
- Connect with fellow students
- Ask questions in course channels
- Share your projects and get feedback

## Accessing the Platform

You can access the course management platform at:
- **ML Zoomcamp**: https://courses.datatalks.club/ml-zoomcamp-2025/
- **Data Engineering Zoomcamp**: https://courses.datatalks.club/de-zoomcamp/
- **MLOps Zoomcamp**: https://courses.datatalks.club/mlops-zoomcamp/

## Registration

1. Visit the registration link for your chosen course
2. Sign up with your email
3. Join the Slack workspace for community access
4. Start tracking your progress

## Need Help?

If you encounter any issues with the platform:
1. Check the course FAQ
2. Ask in the `#platform` channel on Slack
3. Contact support at support@datatalks.club
