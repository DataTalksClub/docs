# General Course Information

Welcome to the general course information section. Here you'll find information that applies to all DataTalks.Club courses.

## About Our Courses

DataTalks.Club offers **free online courses** designed for data professionals. Our zoomcamps are intensive, hands-on programs that cover:

- **Machine Learning** - From fundamentals to deployment
- **Data Engineering** - Building scalable data infrastructure
- **MLOps** - Production ML systems and best practices
- **LLM** - Large Language Models and AI applications
- **AI Development Tools** - Modern tools and workflows

## Course Format

- **Duration**: Varies by course (6-16 weeks)
- **Format**: Self-paced with pre-recorded lectures
- **Cost**: Completely free
- **Certificate**: Available upon completion

## Community

All courses include access to our Slack community where you can:
- Ask questions and get help
- Share your progress and projects
- Connect with fellow learners worldwide

## Getting Started

1. Browse our available courses
2. Check prerequisites for your chosen course
3. Register for the upcoming cohort
4. Join the Slack community
5. Start learning!
