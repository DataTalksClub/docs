# Learning in Public

This page covers the platform UI for submitting learning-in-public links. For what counts as a valid post, the misuse policy, and templates, see [Leaderboard and Learning in Public]({{ '/courses/zoomcamp-logistics/leaderboard/' | relative_url }}).

## Submitting links

When you submit homework, a project, or a peer review, the form has a "Learning in public links" section.

<img src="{{ '/assets/images/course-management-platform/learning-in-public-links.png' | relative_url }}" alt="Learning in public links section in course platform" width="80%">

Add links to your social media posts about what you learned. Each accepted link earns 1 extra point on the leaderboard.

## Link limits per submission type

The platform accepts these limits:

- Homework submissions: up to 7 links.
- Project submissions: up to 14 links.
- Peer reviews: up to 2 links.

## Reporting misuse

Report links in the course Slack channel if they are not the participant's own posts about the course. Examples include documentation pages, other people's content, and unrelated links. The cohort lead can remove their points and disable their ability to earn learning-in-public points.

For the rules and what counts as misuse, see [Leaderboard and Learning in Public]({{ '/courses/zoomcamp-logistics/leaderboard/' | relative_url }}).
