# Course Management Platform

The DataTalks.Club course management platform helps you track your progress, submit homework and projects assignments.

[Visit the platform](https://courses.datatalks.club/)

<img src="{{ '/assets/images/course-management-platform/main.png' | relative_url }}" alt="Course management platform homepage" width="80%">

The homepage displays all available DataTalks.Club courses. Click on any course card to access its dedicated platform.

## Available Courses

The platform hosts multiple zoomcamps:

- ML Zoomcamp - Machine Learning Engneering course
- Data Engineering Zoomcamp - Data pipelines and infrastructure
- MLOps Zoomcamp - Advanced ML Engineering course
- LLM Zoomcamp - AI Engineering course
