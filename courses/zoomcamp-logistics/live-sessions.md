# Live Sessions and Recordings

All DataTalks.Club zoomcamps are built around pre-recorded lectures with occasional live sessions. The cadence and number of live sessions vary per cohort and per course. The exact schedule for the current cohort is on the [course management platform](https://courses.datatalks.club/).

Two kinds of live sessions happen during a cohort:

- Office hours: the cohort lead and instructors answer questions and walk through tricky topics from recent modules.
- Workshops: deep-dives on a specific tool or topic, often delivered by a guest instructor or partner. See [Workshops]({{ '/courses/zoomcamp-logistics/workshops/' | relative_url }}).

## Where they happen

- Stream on the [DataTalks.Club YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/@DataTalksClub).
- Recordings are added to the cohort-specific YouTube playlist as they happen.
- The link to each live stream is announced shortly before in the course Slack channel and on the course Telegram channel.
- No Zoom link, no separate webinar tool, no signup. Just open YouTube at the scheduled time.

For how Slack, Telegram, YouTube, email, and the course platform fit together, see [Communication Channels]({{ '/courses/zoomcamp-logistics/communication/' | relative_url }}).

## Time zones

- Most live sessions are at 17:00 Berlin time (CET / CEST). Occasionally 12:00 noon Berlin time.
- US time zones: typically morning. Asia-Pacific: typically evening or night.

## Asking questions live

Two formats are common:

- YouTube live chat: type questions; the host or co-host reads them out.
- Slido or similar Q+A tool, with the link announced at the start of the session.

If your question is not answered live, post it in the course Slack channel after the session as a thread under the relevant topic.

## Recordings

- Available immediately after the stream ends. YouTube keeps the live broadcast as a regular video on the channel.
- Added to the cohort's YouTube playlist.

You do not need to attend live to be in the live cohort. Watching the recording later does not change your cohort status.
